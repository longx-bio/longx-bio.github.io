LongX is an initiative that aims to empower rising generations within the longevity sector. As the field evolves, the lines between our roles blur.

Regardless of the vision, we need the field to remain young. We aim to:

  • Exchange: by sharing the success and failure stories we wish we heard. From each other, for each other.
  • Educate: by providing young longevity enthusiasts with the up-to-date resources and content we wish we had.
  • Engage: the next generation of leaders, advocates, and curious minds in the fields of aging and longevity, by sharing the opportunities to get involved we wish we were exposed to.

Finally, we would like to say welcome! This is a shared space for interdisciplinary collaboration and creative exploration. Come join us in our Longevity Xploration.
